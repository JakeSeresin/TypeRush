TypeRush Features

⏱️
30s / 1m / 5m modes — Pick a time limit. Your WPM and accuracy are measured when the timer runs out.
🔢
Word Count mode — Choose 10, 25, 50 or 100 words. The test ends once you type them all.
✏️
Custom Text — Paste anything — notes, code, quotes — and type that instead of the built-in word pool.
🗄️
Text Bank — Save multiple custom texts with labels so you can switch between them any time.
🧠
Calibration Engine — After each test the app learns which words slow you down. Those words appear more often in your next test automatically.
🔥
Daily Streak — A calendar grid tracks how many tests you do each day. Click the fire icon to see it.
📈
Live WPM Chart — A small sparkline graph shows your speed changing in real time while you type.
🏆
Results Screen — After every test you see Net WPM, Raw WPM, Accuracy, Errors, Time, and your all-time best.
🕐
History — Click the clock icon to see your last 50 results with date, mode, WPM, and accuracy.
🔤
Categories — Switch the word pool between All, General, Quotes, Tech, or your saved Bank texts.
🔡
Font Size — Three size options (A / A / A) for the typing text — pick what suits your screen.
🔊
Sound Effects — Optional click on correct keys, buzz on errors, chime on finish. Toggle the speaker icon.
⏸️
Pause — Press P or the Pause button. The timer freezes but the rest of the app stays interactive.
🌙
Dark / Light Theme — Toggle with the half-circle icon. Your choice is saved automatically.
🔒
Caps Lock Warning — A yellow bar appears if Caps Lock is on so you never type in uppercase by mistake.
📴
Offline / PWA — Works with no internet. Install from your browser for a native app feel on desktop or mobile.
